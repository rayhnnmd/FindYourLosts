🧭 About Us — FindYourLosts

FindYourLosts is a community-driven lost and found platform designed to help people recover misplaced belongings quickly and safely.

Every day, valuable items like phones, wallets, documents, and personal belongings are lost in public places such as campuses, hostels, libraries, transport hubs, and workplaces. FindYourLosts bridges the gap between people who have lost items and those who have found them, all in one secure and easy-to-use platform.

🔍 What We Do

  • Allow users to post lost or found items with images
  • Enable easy search and filtering by location, category, and item type
  • Maintain item status tracking (open, claimed, returned)
  • Ensure admin moderation to prevent misuse and false listings
  • Provide a secure login system for accountability and safety

🛡️ Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce the stress and time involved in recovering lost belongings by creating a transparent, reliable, and community-focused digital solution.

🌐 Why FindYourLosts?

  • Simple and user-friendly design
  • Secure authentication and role-based access
  • Verified listings through admin approval
  • Designed for real-world use in campuses and public spaces

🚀 Built With

FindYourLosts is built using Flask, MySQL, and modern web technologies, following best practices in backend development, security, and database design.
