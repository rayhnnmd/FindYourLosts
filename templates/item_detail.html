Safety Guidelines

Protect yourself during exchanges

01

Public Meeting Spots

Always meet in well-lit, high-traffic public areas. Police station lobbies, coffee shops, or bank parking lots are ideal choices.

02

Bring a Companion

Whenever possible, bring a friend or family member along. If meeting alone, ensure someone knows exactly where and when you are meeting.

03

Inspect Thoroughly

Take your time to carefully inspect the item before finalizing the exchange. Ensure it matches the description and functions as expected.

04

Private Information

Never share your home address, financial details, or login credentials. Keep communication within the platform whenever possible.

05

Trust Your Instincts

If a situation or user feels suspicious or makes you uncomfortable, don't hesitate to cancel the meeting and leave immediately.
